About

Origins

Boombox is a Ukrainian band founded in 2004 in Kyiv by vocalist Andriy Khlyvnyuk and guitarist Andriy “Mukha” Samoylo. From the start, the group blended funk, hip‑hop, and rock, quickly making its mark on the national music scene.

Success

Their debut album Melomania launched the project, followed by Family Biznes, which went gold. With deep lyrics, catchy melodies, and bold collaborations, Boombox won over a wide audience and built a strong reputation.
